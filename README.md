​## 📊 Relational Database & SQL Portfolio

This repository showcases advanced SQL schema designs and data analytics scripts, with a focus on **data integrity**, **relational constraints**, and **performance optimization**.

---

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1. Training Management System (`lsp-training-management.sql`)

A robust schema designed to manage professional training ecosystems.

* **Logic:** Manages many-to-many relationships between `Trainers` and `Training_Programs`.
* **Integrity:** Implements `AUTO_INCREMENT` primary keys and `CHECK` constraints to ensure data validity (e.g., ensuring experience years are non-negative).

---

### 2. University Student Records (`sscaseit-student-records.sql`)

A centralized management system tailored for institutional academic data, specifically designed for the SS-CASE-IT environment.

* **Logic:** Handles complex student demographics, departmental enrollment, and academic performance tracking.
* **Standards:** Uses optimized data types (`DECIMAL` for currency, `DATE` for timestamps) to ensure high-performance queries and storage efficiency.

---

### 3. Retail Sales Analytics (`megamart-sales-analytics.sql`)

Business intelligence scripts engineered for retail sales reporting and inventory management.

* **Logic:** Features sophisticated aggregate functions (`SUM`, `AVG`, `GROUP BY`) to derive actionable sales insights.
* **Use Case:** Designed to support backend reporting and data visualization for high-volume inventory systems.
